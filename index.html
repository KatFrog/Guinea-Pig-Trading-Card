Guinea Pig

Guinea pigs can learn complex paths to food and can accurately remember a learned path for months.

  • Scientific Name: Cavia porcellus
  • Average Life Span: 4 - 8 years
  • Adult Mass: 700 - 1,200 g (1.5 - 2.6 lbs.)
  • Adult Length: 20 - 25 cm (7.9 - 9.8 in.)

Guinea pigs belong to the rodent family, and were domesticated as early as 5,000 BC. Sadly, there are not good records from that time period, so we don't know a more precise date. Guinea pigs sport a variety of colors (e.g. brown, tan, white, black) and fur that is either very short or very long, but every guinea pig's face has a common look with large eyes on the side and sharp teeth in front.
